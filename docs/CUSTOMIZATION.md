# Customization Guide

## Theme Settings
Use **Online Store → Themes → Customize** and **Theme settings** to configure:
- Colors
- Typography
- Logo and favicon
- Social links
- Cart behavior

## Homepage Sections
The homepage supports these configurable sections:
- Hero
- Trust Bar
- Featured Collections
- Brand Story
- Featured Products
- Testimonials
- Newsletter

You can reorder, hide, and update each section from the Theme Editor.

## Analytics and Pixel
Meta Pixel is optional and only runs when configured by the merchant.

## Content Updates
- Replace placeholder copy with your brand messaging.
- Upload your own imagery and logo assets.
- Link CTA buttons to your chosen collections/pages.
